Building Design System

Your online guide to your refreshed brand.
Everything you need to work with the new identity.

To build a brand, we offer a work plan consisting of the following items:


01. The tone of voice selection. A consistent and clear expression for the company.

What are your favourite brands? You’ll probably have a few. No doubt you can explain quite easily why you love them. You might also be able to describe how those brands behave towards you — and even how they sound. This familiarity and recognition is a result of long-term, consistent brand communication and behaviour. Knowing what to expect from a brand can be part of the reason we love them.

The way you express yourselves and your brand is as important as the way you look. Tone of voice means how we say what we say. Because every word you use in communication contributes to how the brand is perceived.

Different channels have different requirements. The following guidelines cover a principal direction.

Your purpose

This is your ‘Why’ — the reason we exist (besides running a successful global business, of course).

Example:

We help make your world
more comfortable.

Your positioning

How do you reach out to your potential customers and display your product.

Example:

– Bright and confident
– Intriguingly and exceptional
– Distinctly and concise

Your values

These describe what you believe in, and what steers the decisions you make.

Example:

Quality.
Comfort.
Reliability.

Your Brand idea

This sums up all the components of your brand into a single, simple, distinct promise.

Example:

With care for the most important.

We invite you to compose and express your intention, with which the brand will communicate with consumers. Choose the traits that best characterize your company. Try to bring out the most striking properties that you would like to accompany the brand.

This will directly affect the shape and structure of the logo, colors, typography, graphic elements of your brand style. That will allow you to build a single design system for you.

02. Logotype development. Main symbol and mark of the brand.

Logotype is the most important element in your corporate identity and would always be central figure of your brand. We always advise to start with it.

We will develop several logotype options that meet modern design standards. All current technical requirements will be taken into account, such as scaling without distortion. The logotype is often displayed at a small size and should remain legible when viewed on smartphones.

In this step, we demonstrate form and style. Each of the version, will be selected preliminary graphic solutions and fonts corresponding to the structure of the logotype . At this stage it would not be final and will be further developed.

03. The color scheme. We dress the brand in suitable clothes.

Like an outfit, the most appropriate and winning colors would be selected for the logo. Through that, the brand acquires its style and character.

The next step in developing a Design System is to set up a color scheme that matches the main message and tone that the brand has to deliver to the user.

After you approve the logo, we will start painting the brand in colors that will remain the basis of the design system for all brand graphic elements. We'll create a broad yet defined color palette that’s suitable for a range of different applications.

04. Typography is the brand's declaim. Must be clear and attractive.

Typography is an essential component of the visual identity. The identity places a heavy emphasis on contemporary typographic expression, but also ensures clarity of communication doesn’t suffer as a result.

Properly selected font type creates a visual coherence across all of your communication and helps you increase recognition and strengthen the brand.

We pay special attention to typography and aim to connect them securely with the overall direction of brand expression.

05. Establishing communication concept of the brand.

The way you communicate your brand shapes the market’s understanding of who you are and what you do. It’s how you build recognition and trust. And how we make ourselves relevant to customers, partners and employees. Good communication is both clear and consistent.

The communication concept aims to be translated into clear and confident layouts. This is possible by creating the visual guidelines.

The design system will not limit the appearance and shape of the brand, but will only guide and point the way in search of creative solutions.

06. Formation and publication Design System.

Design System is a guide to visual identification. It provides an overview of the design elements that combine to create a brand and instructions for using them.

From the logotype — to the layout rules. It will contain all the materials and resources necessary to ensure that the updated brand remains consistent and understandable for everyone who works with the company's identity.
